To read this file use the arrow keys to move up and down through the text.

To move by word, press w or shift w to go back a word.

When you are finished reading, press escape to quit.

The scree reader you are currently using is called Fenrir. The fenrir key is the numpad insert key. You can use this key in combination with other keys to change settings for speech rate and volume.

Fenrir key combined with left or right arrow will change the speech rate.

Fenrir combined with the up or down arrow will raise or lower the speech volume.
